Step 1–2 · Data

Synthetic dataset of 100,000 diabetic patients with 26 clinical features. Missing values filled with –1. Outliers retained with 8% label-flip noise for realism.

Step 3 · Transformation

Label encoding for 8 categorical columns. Boolean flags to integer. Gaussian feature noise (σ=0.05) on continuous columns during training only.

Step 4–5 · Model

Random Forest (300 estimators, max depth 12, min leaf 10, balanced class weights). 80/20 stratified split. Outperforms Naïve Bayes and Decision Tree baselines.
